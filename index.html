WELCOME TO MY GAMING HISTORY WEBSITE

Hey there! I'm Mohammed and I'm 19 years old. When I first started using computers, I was so obsessed with video games. I started gaming at such an early age (3-4 years old). Now that I'm 19, I'd like to review most (if not all) the games I played throughout my childhood. Have fun!

Starting off from 2000 The first game I've ever played was Doom 1. Doom is an FPS (first person shooting) game which is all about shooting monsters & aliens and completing missions. It was so much fun, but, scary!! Here are some screenshots from Doom1 and the latest version Doom4.

2- Dave

Dave was a very very popular game back then. It's an arcade game all about collecting coins and weapons and finishing tasks to win the game.

3-Zoo Tycoon

Zoo Tycoon is a strategy game where you are the owner of a zoo and it's your responsibility to manage it, customize it, satisfy the visitors and take care of the animals. The game gives you the ability to create and see the result of your creation as if it is in real life! Zoo Tycoon is an old style game now.. if you feel like you wanna play such a game nowadays, I'd recommend Wildlife Park 3 which was more fun for me than Zoo Tycoon View the trailer here:

4- Insaniquarium Delux

Insaniquarium Delux is an arcade game where you take care of a fish tank. It gives you the ability to grow your own fish, name them, fight threats to protect them and even buy/sell them. This is such an exciting child-friendly game that I'd recommend for kids.

5- Mortal Kombat 4

Mortal Kombat in general is the most famous fighting game out there. The game consists of many characters who fight against each other (wether in story mode or single fight). Even though I used to play it when I was a child, I gotta say that it was a wrong decision as the game is extremely agressive and it clearly shouldn't be played by kids. Blood, violence, beheading, burning, etc. That's what Mortal Kombat is all about. Here are some screenshots for Mortal Kombat 4 and the latest version (Mortal Kombat XL)

6- Worms 2

Worms is a funny war game released by Team 17 where two teams (consisted of worms as soilders) fight against each other. The game has a wide variety of weapons, with a special advantage for each one. Worms 2 is a very old game, Worms Ultimate Mayhem is the latest release now. The following are a bunch of screenshots for both Worms 2 & Worms Ultimate Mayhem

7- Mario

Mario of course . A game almost every single one of us has played at least once before. Mario is a 2D game about a hero who goes through many adventures & fights a lot of bosses and eventually saves the princess. Here are some screenshots for Mario Forever

8- Counter Strike 1.6

A game which most guys love and play frequently! Counter Strike is wtihout a doubt the most popular FPS game in history! Even though Counter Strike 1.6 was released sometime around 1997, it's still being played by millions all over the globe. There are multiple other versions of Counter Strike.. the latests are Counter Strike: Source & Counter Strike: Global Offensive. Enjoy the screenshots :)
